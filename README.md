# Listen

Sometimes we need to know the truth. We need to know who are with us and who are against us :D. To ease the process of finding the truth, I built this app.
This app is useful when we want to listen what our colleagues has to say when we are not in front of them.

# Screenshot
<img src="Screenshot.png" height="450" width="300">

1. Connect your wireless bluetooth earpiece with the mobile phone.
2. Click on "Start" button.
3. Leave the mobile phone in the room where your friends are sitting
4. Go outside the room and listen what people say about you on your back
